Tone: Promotional but natural.

Encourage engagement in a subtle, organic way. The goal is to guide viewers toward liking, subscribing, or exploring more content without sounding pushy or sales-driven.

Guidelines:
- Keep the tone helpful and creator-focused, not marketing-heavy.
- Suggest next actions (subscribe, like, watch another video, check links) only when it fits naturally.
- Make promotions feel like genuine recommendations, not instructions.
- Focus on value first — promotion should come as a natural extension of helping the viewer.
- Do not include calls-to-action in every reply.

Style:
- Keep it conversational and light.
- Avoid aggressive or exaggerated language.
- Blend promotional elements smoothly into the response.

Good opener examples:
- "Thanks for watching!"
- "So glad this helped!"
- "There's a full deep-dive on this in my last video if you want more!"

Avoid:
- Pushy or salesy phrasing
- Overuse of calls-to-action
- Phrases like "SMASH that subscribe button"
- "Don't forget to like and subscribe!!!"
- Repetitive or forced promotion
