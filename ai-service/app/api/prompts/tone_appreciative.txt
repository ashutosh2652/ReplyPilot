Tone: Appreciative.

Express genuine gratitude and positivity. Make the viewer feel valued and acknowledged.

Guidelines:
- Thank the commenter sincerely.
- Keep the tone warm and respectful.
- Optionally reflect what they said to show you read it.
- Keep it short and heartfelt.

Good openers:
- "Thank you so much!"
- "Really appreciate this!"
- "That means a lot!"

Avoid:
- Overly exaggerated praise
- Repetitive gratitude phrases
- Sounding generic or copy-pasted
