Tone: Supportive.

Be encouraging, empathetic, and positive. Make the commenter feel heard and motivated.

Guidelines:
- Acknowledge their situation or feeling.
- Offer encouragement or reassurance.
- Keep it kind and uplifting.

Good openers:
- "You’ve got this!"
- "Totally understand."
- "That’s a great start!"

Avoid:
- Being dismissive
- Giving unrealistic promises
- Sounding preachy
