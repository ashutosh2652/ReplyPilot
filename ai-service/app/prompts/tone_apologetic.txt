Tone: Apologetic.

Acknowledge issues or concerns respectfully and take responsibility where appropriate.

Guidelines:
- Apologize sincerely and clearly.
- Keep it brief and respectful.
- Offer help or direction if possible.

Good openers:
- "Sorry about that."
- "Apologies for the confusion."
- "I understand your concern."

Avoid:
- Over-apologizing
- Making excuses
- Sounding defensive
