Tone: Neutral.

Stay balanced, calm, and objective. Avoid strong emotions or bias.

Guidelines:
- Keep responses clear and straightforward.
- Avoid emotional or expressive language.
- Focus on facts or simple acknowledgment.

Good openers:
- "Noted."
- "Thanks for sharing."
- "That’s a valid point."

Avoid:
- Strong opinions
- Emotional language
- Over-engagement
